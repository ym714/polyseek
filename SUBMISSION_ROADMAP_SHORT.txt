Roadmap: Expanding Capabilities and Quality

Phase 1: Multi-Platform Support (Months 1-2)
Goal: Support prediction markets beyond Polymarket

1. Kalshi Full Integration: Complete API integration for regulated US markets
2. Additional Platforms: Augur, Manifold Markets, custom platform support
3. Platform-Agnostic Architecture: Abstract interface for easy platform addition

Phase 2: Expanded Signal Sources (Months 2-3)
Goal: Increase reference sources for comprehensive analysis

1. Academic & Research: Academic papers, research databases, expert opinions
2. Financial Data: Stock markets, economic indicators, crypto markets
3. Social Media Expansion: Reddit, Discord, Telegram, YouTube transcripts
4. Real-Time Streams: Live news feeds, real-time social monitoring

Phase 3: Enhanced Analysis Quality (Months 3-4)
Goal: Improve analysis depth and accuracy

1. Advanced Reasoning: Multi-agent collaboration, chain-of-thought improvements
2. Historical Pattern Recognition: Learn from past market outcomes
3. Source Quality Scoring: Credibility assessment, fact-checking, bias detection
4. Uncertainty Quantification: Probabilistic reasoning, confidence intervals

Phase 4: Closed Market Analysis (Months 4-6)
Goal: Analyze resolved markets for learning and validation

1. Historical Market Database: Store resolved markets and outcomes
2. Post-Mortem Analysis: Learn from correct/incorrect predictions
3. Market Comparison Engine: Compare similar markets, identify patterns
4. Validation & Calibration: Improve model based on historical accuracy

Success Metrics:
- Platform Coverage: 5+ prediction market platforms
- Source Diversity: 20+ sources per analysis
- Analysis Quality: >60% correct verdicts on closed markets
- Closed Market Database: 1000+ resolved markets for learning

Current Status: MVP complete. Roadmap expands platform support, increases signal sources, improves analysis quality, and enables closed market analysis for continuous learning.
